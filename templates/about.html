Welcome to Bala Skating Academy!

I am Bala Subramanian, a passionate and experienced Skating Coach based in Thirumangalam, Madurai, with over 15 years of dedicated involvement in sports and physical education. I am the founder of Bala Skating Academy, where I train aspiring skaters across various age groups, helping them build skills, confidence, and discipline through structured coaching.

My professional journey began as a Physical Education Teacher at Thuraiyur Sowdambika Matric School. From 2010 to 2015, I conducted skating classes at Agrinai Apartment, Madurai, laying the foundation for what would become Bala Skating Academy. Since 2015, I have been serving as a Skating Coach at Velammal School, where I continue to train and mentor young talents, guiding them in competitions and building their physical fitness and sportsmanship.

With a strong background in education and a lifelong passion for physical training, I strive to make skating both accessible and enjoyable for every student.
