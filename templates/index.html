About Us

Where Champions Learn to Skate

Bala Skating Academy is a dedicated inline skating school located near Mahendra Car Company, Kappalur. We provide structured coaching that nurtures young talent from their first glide to competitive excellence.

Our academy emphasises discipline, fitness, and fun — ensuring every student not only masters skating skills but also builds character and confidence that lasts a lifetime.

  • Competition preparation & support
